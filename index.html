Make your cake and have it too.

We came together after countless hours poured over family cookbooks, frustrated that our culinary legacy had been ruined by sauce splatters, butter smudges, and sugary stains. If only there was a way to preserve these recipes for generations. Past attempts at digitizing our recipe boxes ended up with floppy disk, zip drives, and burned cd's filled with recipes that could no longer be accessed as older technology became obsolete. Our new online database allows users to create, edit, and store any recipe from any device with internet access. No longer do you need to worry about downloading and printing off files. Edit once, store always. Share your favorites with friends and family!
